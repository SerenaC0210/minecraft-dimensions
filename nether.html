Welcome to the Nether

A fiery dimension that is filled with lava lakes. It's packed with rare resources, unique blocks, and important structures. Bring the right gear and watch your step!

  • No daylight cycle

To get to the nether, a nether portal must be built. A standard frame for a nether portal is 14 blocks of obsidian. This structure must be lit by fire to activate.

Nether Mobs

The nether is packed with hostile mobs that are way tougher than most overworld enemies.

This on the right is a Ghast. These ghost-like mobs float around aimlessly and shoot fireballs at players.

Nether Structures

These are dangerous but rewarding places to explore. They are filled with rare loot and powerful enemies. Some of these structures are important for progressing through the game.

This is part of a nether fortress.

Nether Ores

These ores are rarer and often harder to mine. The most common are quartz and gold.
